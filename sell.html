What can I sell?

Environmentally friendly and sustainably harvested agro and forestry products; fruits and berries, tea to turmeric, peppermint, cardamone and wellness products, essential oil, soap bars, ensence and also from cottage crafts and fashion, high land coffee, herbal medicines even the best yarsagumba and other authentic Himalayan products can be easy listed ( see The Cagtegory for more) Small enterprises to larger manufactures, farmers to traders all can enjoy their inter sales easy. The products we promote are nature friendly products and various local communities products will be encouraged where we believe the most of hand made products are always best and the Himalayan Green has build your sales and promotion so friendly.

I am a producer based outside Nepal, can I become your member?

Can I sell to Himalayan Green directly in bulk?

Yes! if you do not prefer managing an e-commerce platform, we can collaborate with you by buying in bulk and listing your products by rebranding them. Either way, please fill out the membership application to get started.

Absolutely. Once you become a member, we will train you on how to use our platform. The training material is publicly available in our resources section.
